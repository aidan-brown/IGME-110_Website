Castlevania: Symphony of the Night

Released in 1997, Castlevania: Symphony of the Night featured a large non-linear map with many upgrades hidden throughout. Unlike Metroid, Castlevania also implemented an RPG system with experience points and leveling up that gave useful bonuses alongside the different items you obtained (“Castlevania: Symphony of the Night (Game)”).
