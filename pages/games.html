My Favorite Games!

In no specific order

Portal 2:

An interesting, challenging and funny puzzle game from Valve that utilizes portals and physics to solve the puzzles.

Release Date: April 19, 2011
Developer: Valve Corporation
Publisher: Valve Corporation

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim:

A large scale, fantasy RPG from the Bethesda Game Studios in which you play as a dragonborn (Dragon blood in your veins) trying to stop the end of the world.

Release Date: November 11, 2011
Developer: Bethesda Game Studios
Publisher: Bethesda Softworks

Divinity: Original Sin II:

An interesting, turn-based fantasy RPG from Larian Studios that is based upon table top RPG games such as Dungeons & Dragons.

Release Date: September 14, 2017
Devloper: Larian Studios
Publisher: Bandi Namco Entertainment

Just Cause 3:

A huge open-world action game from Avalanche Studios that follows Rico Rodriguez as he attempts to liberate his homeland, Medici, from a dictatorship.

Release Date: December 1, 2015
Devloper: Avalanche Studios
Publisher: Square Enix

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild:

An amazing, beautiful, open-world adventure game by Nintendo that follows Link as he tries to figure out his purpose after waking up from a 100 year nap.

Release Date: March 3, 2017
Devloper: Nintendo
Publisher: Nintendo

Pokemon FireRed:

A turn-based rpg by Game Freak in which you pit two monsters against each other in order to be the very best like no one ever was.

Release Date: September 7, 2004
Devloper: Game Freak
Publisher: The Pokemon Company/Nintendo

Super Smash Bros:

A "casual" fighting game by Nintendo in which you pit various Nintendo characters (and others) against each other.

Release Date (Original): January 21, 1999
Devloper (Original): HAL Laboratory
Release Date (Latest): November 21, 2014
Devloper (Latest): Bandai Namco Studios
Publisher: Nintendo
